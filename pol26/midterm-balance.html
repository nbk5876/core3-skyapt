How thin the margin is

Republicans can afford to lose only a small handful of seats. Democrats need a net gain of three to flip the chamber. Forecasters rate the House a genuine toss-up, with redistricting fights in Texas, California, North Carolina, Ohio, Missouri, Virginia and Utah reshaping the battlefield.
